# Batch-01 Anti-Patterns

- Do not copy a source brand's palette, shell chrome, or illustration style without the concept that makes it coherent.
- Do not add faux desktop or docs framing unless semantic structure, focus order, and scannability remain strong.
- Do not ship screenshot-heavy sections that collapse into unreadable noise on mobile.
- Do not use bento or modular grids when the content hierarchy is weak or every card feels equally important.
- Do not add canvas or WebGL effects only to signal sophistication; they need a real narrative or proof role.
- Do not lean on jokes, launch banners, or promo overlays if they distract from the main task.
- Do not animate counters, galleries, or tabs so aggressively that they outrun comprehension.
- Do not make mobile a pure collapse of desktop; reprioritize instead.
